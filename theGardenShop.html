About us:

Based in Portarlington, Co. Laois, TheGardenShop.ie was founded in 2011 with the principle of offering a wide range of garden products for the best value in Ireland. Together with our low-cost model & strong focus on customer service, our online garden shop quickly expanded in the Irish market. At TheGardenShop.ie, we have something for everyone, whether you are a beginner who wants to start out with a few small pots and trays on your windowsill, the 'green fingered' expert or professional landscape gardener, we have the tools for you.
